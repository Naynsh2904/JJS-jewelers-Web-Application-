Jewellery Care Guide

Your jewellery is precious and deserves proper care to maintain its beauty and shine for years. Follow these simple care tips to keep your pieces looking as good as new.

Jewellery Care Guide

  1. Keep Jewellery Dry -

    Avoid water, perfumes, and chemicals to prevent tarnishing.

  2. Store Properly –

    Use a soft pouch or a jewellery box with separate compartments.

  3. Remove Before Activities -

    Take off jewellery before swimming, exercising, or cleaning.

  4. Clean Regularly –

    Use a soft cloth for polishing; mild soap and water for deep cleaning.

  5. Handle with Care -

    Avoid dropping or hitting jewellery against hard surfaces.

  6. Separate Storage

    Store different pieces separately to prevent scratches.

  7. Use Anti-Tarnish Strips –

    Helps keep silver jewellery shiny.

  8. Avoid Direct Sunlight

    Prolonged exposure can fade gemstones.

  9. Check for Loose Stones -

    Get prongs and clasps checked regularly.

  10. Professional Cleaning -

    Have your jewellery professionally cleaned once a year.
